Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? Here are some quick answers about my work, tools, availability, and how to get in touch. If you don’t find what you’re looking for, feel free to reach out!

Answer: This website was built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I also used React.js for dynamic components, and Tailwind CSS for fast and responsive design. It’s hosted on GitHub Pages (or Netlify/Vercel depending on your hosting).

Answer: You can use the Contact form on this site, or reach out to me via email at [your email]. I’m also active on LinkedIn and GitHub—links are in the footer.

Answer: Yes, I’ve worked on group projects during college and contributed to open-source collaborations on GitHub. I’ve also handled freelance projects, which involved client meetings, requirement gathering, and delivering solutions based on their needs.
